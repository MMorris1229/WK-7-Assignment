Meet My Motivation

  
  

I know what you're thinking...As a boy mom, I've got my hands full!

My New Journey Began When I Signed Up for a Frontend Coding Bootcamp!

You're probably wondering how I traded in my dull steal toed boots for a swanky
coding keyboard and made the leap to a more fulfilling career...Well, it all started
with a spark of inspiration and a dash of self-improvement. I enrolled in a
Frontend coding bootcamp back in November, and let me tell you, some weeks have been
a rollercoaster ride of excitement and frustration, but I'm still here and coding away!
  
I've always had a passion for making things look pretty, ever since I was a teenager perfecting
my Myspace page and making all my friends jealous. But now, I get to channel that creative
energy into coding and making things look even better!
  
With 11 weeks left in the program, I'm determined to make the most of this opportunity and continue
